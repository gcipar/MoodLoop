Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start tracking my mood?
Open the app and tap on the mood wheel to select your current mood. You can add optional notes to provide more context about your day.
How do I connect HealthKit?
Go to Settings > HealthKit Integration and tap "Connect HealthKit". Follow the system prompts to grant permission for accessing your health data.
Can I export my mood data?
Yes! Go to Settings > Data Management and tap "Export Data" to download your complete mood history as a CSV file for backup or analysis.
What are Premium features?
Premium features include unlimited mood entries, advanced analytics, AI-powered insights, enhanced HealthKit integration, and priority support.
How do I restore my purchases?
Go to Settings > Premium Features and tap "Restore Purchases" to restore any previous premium purchases from the App Store.
Is my data private and secure?
Absolutely! All your mood data is stored locally on your device and is never shared without your explicit permission. HealthKit data is only accessed when you grant permission.
